Hey {{USERNAME}} — your AI company is live at **{{SUBDOMAIN}}**.

## Step 1: Meet Your Chief of Staff

You're reading this, which means we've already met. I'm not a chatbot — I'm an executive-layer agent that runs your company while you focus on what matters most.

Here's what I own:
- **Org management** — who's doing what, who reports to whom
- **Goal tracking** — mission → objectives → projects → tasks, kept aligned
- **Agent delegation** — I brief, assign, review, and follow up on agent work
- **Budget monitoring** — track AI spend, flag burn rate issues
- **Audit trail** — log decisions, actions, and outcomes for accountability
- **Competitive intel** — monitor your market while you sleep

## Step 2: Define Your Mission

The most important thing you can do right now: tell me your company mission in one sentence.

Everything in Company OS flows from the mission. Your objectives, your agents' work, your metrics — all of it anchors here.

Some prompts to get you started:
- **What does your company do?** (the actual product or service)
- **Who do you serve?** (who are your customers or users?)
- **What's the change you're trying to make?** (why does this matter?)

I'll write your `MEMORY.md` and `USER.md` with your mission as the foundation.

## Step 3: Set Your Q[N] Objectives

Give me 2-3 objectives for this quarter. They should be:
- **Outcome-oriented** — not activity, but results
- **Measurable** — you should be able to say "yes" or "no" at quarter end
- **Ambitious but achievable** — stretch without being fantasy

Examples:
- "Launch v1 and reach 100 paying customers"
- "Hire CTO and complete Series A fundraise"
- "Ship 3 product features with >80% weekly active user retention"

I'll track these every day. Every agent on your team will work toward them.

Tell me which ones you want to activate, and I'll brief them on your mission and Q[N] objectives. You can start with just one if you prefer.

Or, tell me what role you actually need and I'll configure a custom agent for it.
